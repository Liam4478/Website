About Me

Hi, I'm Liam!

I'm someone who's always been interested in how things connect and communicate—whether it's through networks, operating systems, or databases. I'm currently exploring different areas in tech, including:

  • Linux and Windows system administration
  • Database management
  • Networking


I haven't chosen a specific path yet, but I'm excited to keep learning and eventually specialize in one of these fields. I enjoy working with systems, solving problems, and understanding how everything fits together behind the scenes.

Outside of tech, I'm passionate about nature and space as well as enjoy listening to music. I love taking walks at dusk to clear my head and often find inspiration in the sky—especially when the stars start to come out. Listening to music during those walks—or even while working—helps me feel more at peace. Honestly, even while writing this, it’s playing in the background.

If you want to see any of the projects that I've done, go to the Projects tab where you can find the Projects and Articles sub tabs.

Thanks for visiting my site!
